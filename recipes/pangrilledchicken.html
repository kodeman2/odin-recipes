Pan Grilled Chicken

Description.

pan sauce—made with just a handful of ingredients and in a matter of minutes—can look and taste nearly as rich as a classic, labor-intensive French sauce. The base of a pan sauce is the fond, or browned bits, clinging to the bottom of the skillet after sautéing or searing meat, poultry, or fish.

Ingridents.

  • 2 chicken breast
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato
  • Salt cube seasoning
  • Spring onions
  • Capsicum green and red
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken for 24hours. Grill using grill pan, and leave warm in the oven.
  2. Wash and cut viggies and sauté. And some stock or wine to the viggies. Season and check taste.
  3. Plate viggies and top chicken. Drizzle some juice on top and garnish with spring onion leaves.
