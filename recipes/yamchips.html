yam chips Recipes

Description.

Yam which is a tuber crop comes in different varieties. While are considered for some specific usage, others fit into almost any dish. Yam could be eaten Fried, boiled, roasted, pounded, grinded, mashed, streamed among others.

Ingridents.

  • 1 tuber of white yam
  • Oil for frying
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel, cut yam into large batonnate
  2. Soak in plenty water to free up starch.
  3. Drain with colander
  4. Pat dry with kitchen towel
  5. Heat frying oil. When hot for frying. Drop your yams in batches a and fry for 8mins. Repeat on others until complete.
  6. put little salt
  7. add pepper sauce
