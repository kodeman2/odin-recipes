Vanilla Cake

Description.

To start, note that both white and yellow cakes are vanilla cakes. ... It actually just refers to the flavor of a vanilla custard (aka the classic French way of making ice cream from cream and egg yolks). French vanilla cake mimics a custard flavor by including egg yolks in the batter, making it a yellow cake.

Ingridents.

  • 2 cup flour
  • 1 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla flavour
  • 1 1/4 cup margarine
  • 1 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 4 eggs

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven.
  2. Sift all all dry ingreidents.
  3. Mix margarine and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time and combine.
  5. Add flour and baking powder together. and mix. Add milk and vanilla and fold.
  6. Add flour and fold.
  7. Pour into geased baking pan and bake.
  8. Check if done, using a toothpick or cake taster.
